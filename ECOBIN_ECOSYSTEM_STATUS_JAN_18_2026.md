## 💡 Technical Insights

### 1. Concentrated Gap Strategy

**Principle**: One primal handles one responsibility

**Implementation**:
- **BearDog**: ALL crypto operations (Pure Rust)
- **Songbird**: TLS protocol + HTTP gateway (Pure Rust)
- **Others**: Pure IPC (Unix sockets, JSON-RPC)

**Result**: Perfect separation of concerns!

---

### 2. Capability-Based Architecture

**Principle**: Discover capabilities at runtime

**Implementation**:
- `CryptoProvider` trait (abstraction)
- `discover_crypto_provider()` (runtime discovery)
- `UnixSocketCryptoProvider` (implementation)
- `MockCryptoProvider` (testing)

**Result**: Testable, flexible, TRUE PRIMAL!

---

### 3. Pure Rust Crypto Stack

**Components**:
- `ed25519-dalek`: Digital signatures
- `x25519-dalek`: Key exchange
- `chacha20poly1305`: AEAD encryption
- `blake3`: Hashing (with `pure` feature!)
- `hmac`, `sha2`, `sha3`: MACs and hashing

**Result**: 100% Pure Rust, zero C dependencies!
